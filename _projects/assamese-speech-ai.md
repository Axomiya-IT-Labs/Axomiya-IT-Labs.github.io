## The Problem

Voice interfaces (like Siri or Google Assistant) fail to understand native Assamese accents and regional dialects.

## The Idea

Fine-tune OpenAI Whisper or Wav2Vec2 on crowdsourced Assamese voice data to create a lightweight, open-source Speech-to-Text model that runs locally on smartphones.
