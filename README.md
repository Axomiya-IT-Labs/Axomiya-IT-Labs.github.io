## Welcome

**Axomiya IT Labs** is an open-source community dedicated to making Artificial Intelligence, technology, and digital skills accessible to everyone — regardless of age, background, language, or location.

We believe knowledge should never be limited by geography or opportunity. Whether you're a student, teacher, developer, entrepreneur, creator, or simply curious about technology — **you belong here.**

---

## The Internet Changed Everything

For the first time in history, knowledge is available to almost everyone. Whether you live in a small village in Assam or a major city anywhere in the world, you can learn from the same books, courses, videos, documentation, and communities.

Information is no longer scarce. Opportunity is no longer limited by location.

The biggest difference today isn't *who has access to knowledge* — it's **who chooses to learn, build, and create.**

---

## Degrees Help. Curiosity Builds.

A college degree creates opportunities — structure, mentorship, recognition. But learning doesn't stop after graduation.

Many engineers, founders, designers, researchers, and creators continue learning throughout their lives — often through curiosity, experimentation, books, and continuous practice, not formal instruction alone.

The lesson isn't that formal education doesn't matter. The lesson is that **continuous learning matters even more** — and the internet gives everyone a place to start.

---

## Welcome to the AI Era

AI is transforming nearly every industry. It helps people:

| | |
|---|---|
| Learn faster | Automate repetitive work |
| Write code | Analyze data |
| Build businesses | Design products |
| Create content | Solve complex problems |

Today, one person equipped with AI can accomplish work that once required entire teams. Yet millions of people — including many in Assam — still don't know what AI is or how it can improve their everyday lives.

**That's the problem we're solving.**

## From Consumers to Creators

Almost everyone owns a smartphone. Many use it only for entertainment — but technology can do far more. It can help you learn valuable skills, build software, start businesses, earn online, solve local problems, join global communities, and contribute to open source.

Our mission, simply put:

> **Turn Consumers into Creators.**

## Open Source First

Knowledge grows when it is shared. Open source lets people from different backgrounds, cultures, and experiences collaborate, improve ideas, and build technology together — better than any individual could build alone.

Whenever possible, our work is **free, open source, community-driven, transparent, educational, and collaborative.**

---

## Built in Assamese & English

Language should never be a barrier to learning. One of our core goals is quality technology education in both English and Assamese.

**Currently supported:** English · Assamese (অসমীয়া)
More languages will be added as our community grows.

---

## Why Open Source?

Open source is more than writing code — it's sharing knowledge and helping others learn by doing. Every project becomes a classroom; every contribution becomes an opportunity to learn.

Fixing a typo, improving documentation, reporting a bug, translating content, building a feature — every contribution has value. **That's why we build in public.**

## Our Vision
